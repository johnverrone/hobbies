- Drill: 3-ball lag putting from 20/30/40 feet, 10 min per practice session
- Goal: Cut 3-putt rate from ~20% to under 10%

**Priority 2: Short Game — Chipping Consistency**
- Commit to one method: bump-and-run with 8-iron for most chips
- Drill: 50 chips per session to a landing zone, not the hole

**Priority 3: Course Management**
- Rule: Tee shot with 3-wood (not driver) on any hole under 400 yards
- Rule: Play to the fat part of the green on approach, not the pin
