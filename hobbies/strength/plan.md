# Strength Training Plan

## Current Mesocycle: GZCLP Strength Block (March–May 2026)

**Program:** GZCLP (Greyskull Linear Progression variant)
**Block goal:** Strength — drive 5RM PRs on squat, bench, and deadlift
**Deload:** Every 4 weeks or when unable to progress for 3 sessions

**Weekly structure:**
- Monday: Upper A (Bench + Row + OHP accessory)
- Wednesday: Lower A (Squat + Deadlift)
- Friday: Upper B (OHP + Row + Bench accessory)

**Progression:** +5 lbs per session on upper; +10 lbs per session on lower
**Failure protocol:** Drop to 3x3, attempt 3x5 again next session

## Milestone Targets (end of 2026)

- Squat: 300 lbs (1RM)
- Bench: 225 lbs (1RM)
- Deadlift: 365 lbs (1RM)
- OHP: 155 lbs (1RM)
